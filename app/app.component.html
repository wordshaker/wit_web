About

We are a monthly meetup for women from all area’s of technology and those with an emerging interest in technology. The events range from talks to activities, and we try to keep them accesible to all levels. Don't be scared! You don't need to be a technical genius.

We also support anyone wanting to do a talk. First time? We will support you every step of the way in any way we can.

As we are celebrating the women in our industry, and not aiming to exclude, the event has a “Bring a Bloke” scheme, meaning each female attendee can bring one male friend if they want to. This keeps a female majority at all times.

Women in Tech is also on many social media sites which you can visit using the links below. Please sign up for our events on the Meetup website so we know how many pizza's to buy.
